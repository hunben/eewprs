The Dance of the Expansion of Mind – Excerpt B
10 November 1978, Calcutta

Everything in this universe is characterized by certain colours. Knowingly or unknowingly human beings are attracted by these colours. Some such as green and chocolate brown are very pleasing to the human eye. Perhaps you know that these two colours have been selected for those dancing the kaośikii dance. Colour has a tremendous influence on everything. When the human mind becomes strong enough not to be affected by any colour, it is called vaerágya [renunciation].

10 November 1978, Calcutta
