Excerpt from Prabháta Saḿgiita 919
30 September 1983, Kolkata

Áj bhát́ir t́áne priitir gáne tariikháni hacche cáláte,
Ei álo-jhará sudháy bhará paráń-káŕá cándra nishiithe.

Madhumálaiṋca, Kolkata, 30/9/83

Today, rowing downstream, singing the song of love, I glide the boat along,
On this effulgent and enchanting moonlit night filled with nectar divine.

30 September 1983, Kolkata
