Svádhyáya
1986, Kolkata

To read out and explain Ananda Marga scriptures before assembled sádhakas is called aopadhyáyik svádhyáya. Ananda Marga [[dharma]] scriptures means Subháśita Saḿgraha (all parts), Ánanda Vacanámrtam (all parts), and the books Namámi Krśńa Sundaram and Namah Shiváya Shántáya. The rest of the Ananda Marga literature is considered the associate scriptures. Ánanda Sútram is our philosophical treatise, and Caryácarya (all parts) is our social treatise.
