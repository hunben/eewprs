The “Concluding Words” were headed as such and came at the end of the first editions, in different languages, of Caryácarya Part 1. But they had no heading and came at the beginning of the first Bengali edition of Caryácarya Part 2.

Concluding Words
1956, Jamalpur

Utilizing the vibrations created for eternal time
by Shrii Shrii Ánandamúrtijii through jánusparsha
and varábhaya mudrás, take
yourself and the entire universe
ahead along the path of
all-round welfare.
Oṋḿ shánti!

1956, Jamalpur
