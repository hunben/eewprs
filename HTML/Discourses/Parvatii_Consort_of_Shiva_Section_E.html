PÁRVATII, THE YOGINII

About seven thousand years ago a great yogi, Sadáshiva, systematised different branches of yoga. His spouse, Párvatii, was also a yogini, and for the welfare of the entire human society Lord Shiva and Párvatii tried their best to create a practical cult.
