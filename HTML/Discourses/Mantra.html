Notes:

The author spoke on the subject of mantra, commenting on the shloka, Mananát tárayet yastu sah mantrah parikiirttitah [“A mantra is that collection of sounds which, when meditated upon, leads to liberation”]. Much of the tape is unintelligible. Efforts to transcribe it are going on.
