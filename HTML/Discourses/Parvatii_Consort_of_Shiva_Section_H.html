PÁRVATII, THE YOGINII

And Párvatii, the spouse of Shiva, invented lalita mármika, which means “voice of the inner spirit”. The dance used for kiirtana(1) is lalita mármika. It represents the inner voice of the spiritual aspirant. The main thing in lalita mármika is the expression through mudrá [gesture].

Footnotes

(1) Collective singing of the Lord’s name. –Trans.
