Iigal to Iikśańaka/ Iikśańika/ Iikśańiká (Discourse 8) (9)

[95] Iikśa

[96] The meaning of the verbal root iikś is “to scrutinize”. Iikś + ac = iikśa. Iikśa means “one who scrutinizes” [...] pariikśá [test] (pari – iikśá) is used for “to scrutinize [...] scrutinize exhaustively” is sam - iikśá = samiikśá (to observe, [...] that direction, is prati – iikśá = pratiikśá [awaiting]. [...] disregard” will be upa – iikśá = upekśá. Actually, iikśá in this sense is pra – iikśá = prekśá [observation] (prekśágrha means “cinema hall”).
