[25] Iliisha

[26] Il + iisha = iliisha. Il means “to go down into [...] “king”. The etymological meaning of iliisha is “the king of water”. Colloquially, it refers to a certain kind of fish. Since those iliisha which unwittingly get caught in [...] they are called viliisha (vi + iliisha = viliisha). In Bengal, different people spell iliisha in different ways. This is not desirable. The correct spelling is iliisha because it is according to its derivation.
