[152] Kuiṋjara

[153] Kuiṋja + ra + d́a = kuiṋjara. Kuiṋja means something [...] under the earth. In common usage Kuiṋjara means the following: A tusker [a tusked elephant].

[154] The word kuiṋjara is also used for a person [...] Vidyákuiṋjara , for example.

[156] Another meaning of the word kuiṋjara is abhraḿlehii giri (a high mountain; literally, a sky-kissing mountain).

[157] Another meaning of the word kuiṋjara is brick.

[158] Another meaning of the word kuiṋjara is ́wavy hair’.

[159] Another meaning of the word kuiṋjara is country.
