Kup to Kula (Discourse 32) (6)

[52] Kurúpa

[53] Ku + rúpa . The etymological meaning of kurúpa is “bad-looking”, “ugly”. The colloquial meaning of kurúpa is “clay-pot” or something made of clay.

[54] The second meaning of the word kurúpa is “clay doll”.

[55] The third meaning of the word kurúpa is “clay toy”. In general the word kurúpa can be used for any earthen vessel.
