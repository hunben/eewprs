Kukśa to Kuntala (Discourse 30) (10)

[16] Kutanu

[17] Ku + tan +un = kutanu . Its etymological meaning [...] is “physical body”.

[19] In colloquial usage kutanu means: An ugly or [...] that person who has such a body.

[20] Just as the quinquelemental [...] that mental body, is called kutanu . In other words kutanu means “sinful mind”. The word kutanu in this sense is in many cases [...] them on their stoves. They are kutanu for each other.

[21] When they accidentally come [...] and the daughter-in-law are kutanu for each other.

[22] If a visarga [aspirant letter] is added to the word kutanu , then its meaning becomes “Kubera”, the god of wealth. Kutanu means “Kubera” and atanu means “Cupid”, or the god of love.
