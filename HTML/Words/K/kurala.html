[14] Kurála

[15] Kura + alac (kalac) = kurála . Etymologically kurála means “a speical kind of fast animal”. Colloquially kurála refers to a rare kind of horse [...] with dark black legs. These kurála horses are not found in today’s world. Nowadays they are as extinct as the dodo.

[16] The kurála horse was a high-bred species. [...] the language of those days, kurála used to mean “petticoat”.
