Kulaka to Kulottha (Discourse 33) (5)

[49] Kulát́a

[50] Kula + at́a [...] The etymological meaning of kulát́a is “that which roams near the kula or shore”. The colloquial meaning of kulát́a is “prawn.”

[51] Kulát́a + kan = kulát́aka. Kulát́aka means “large prawn”.

[52] Kulát́a refers to all kinds of crusaceans [...] found in certain places in the sea.
