[31] Kut́heru

[32] Kut́her + uń = kut́heru . The etymological meaning of kut́heru is “that which is stable”, [...] surroundings”. The colloquial meanings of kut́heru are: Well water.

[33] The other meaning of kut́heru is “the breeze from a fan”, whether a hand fan or an electric fan.
