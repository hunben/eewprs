[33] Kuruvilva

[34] Kuru + vil + van = kuruvilva . The meaning of the root verb [...] The etymological meaning of kuruvilva is “that which is pierced”, [...] hole in it”. Colloquially kuruvilva refers to any of the nine gems, but more specifically the ruby.
