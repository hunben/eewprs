[276] Kaḿsári

[277] Kans + ac = kaḿsa + ari = kaḿsári. Kaḿsasya arih ityarthe [...] etymological meaning of the word kaḿsári is “that which, when obtained, [...] one’s desires”, that is, kámsári means Parama Puruśa. Colloquially:

[279] 2) Kaḿsa was the king of [...] Krśńa, thus colloquially Kaḿsári means Krśńa.
