Kit́i to Kiisha (Discourse 26) (3)

[67] The etymological meaning of the word kiit́aghna is “killer of pest”, [...] medicine or poison that kills insects.

[68] The second meaning of the word kiit́aghna is sulphur.

[69] The third meaning of kiit́aghna is copper sulphate or the poison of blue vitriol.
