Methods of Salutation (2)

[5] Do not shake hands with anyone, because it is unhygienic, and do not do kurnish [a kind of court salute signifying [...] you are not anyone’s slave. Kurnish is a symbol of slavery, so as a form of salutation it is strictly forbidden.
