[46] Kiicaka

[47] The verbal root kiic means to make a sound by touching. Etymologically kiicaka means “that which produces [...] bamboo or the slip of a bamboo.

[48] If a bamboo is very hollow, [...] hollow bamboo is also called kiicaka .

[49] If someone touches fire, they will at once let out a shriek. So one name of fire is kiicaka .

[50] If a demon, ghoul or carnivore [...] plaintive shriek. Therefore the word kiicaka is used for an demon, ghoul or carnovore.

[51] Kiicaka is the name of a mythical demon.
