Kabala to Kaḿsári (Discourse 20) (4)

[131] 5) Kákabhúśańd́ii: In the Puranic story Kákabhúśańd́ii knew the past, present and future. Thus, in olden times any profoundly wise person used to be called kákabhúśańd́ii out of respect. Nowadays the word kákabhúśańd́ii is mostly used in a joking way.
