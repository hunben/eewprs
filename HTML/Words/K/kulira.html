Kulaka to Kulottha (Discourse 33) (4)

[81] Kulira/Kuliira

[82] Kuli + rá + d́a [...] d́a . Both spellings, kulira and kuliira , are correct. Kuliira refers to saltwater or sea crabs.

[83] It also means large, saltwater [...] red oysters, also called rakta kulira . Kuliraka is derived by adding the suffix kan to kulira ; it means “a large ocean crab”.
