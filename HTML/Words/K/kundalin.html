Kut́t́ii to Kuńd́u (Discourse 29) (7)

[138] Kuńd́alin

[139] Kuńd́ala + in = kuńd́alin . The etymological meaning of kuńd́alin is “connected with kuńd́alii (coil)”.

[140] The colloquial meanings of kuńd́alin are: Snake.

[141] Kuńd́alin refers to the Tantrik shakti [divine creative force] or the shakti cult.

[142] Kuńd́alin also refers to pigeon pea [...] ámakaláya-kuńd́alina [green peas].

[143] Kuńd́alina [...] kuńd́alina , that is, the word kuńd́alin alone means cháná [...] means “bluffing”.
