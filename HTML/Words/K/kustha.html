Leprosy (3)

[1] Symptoms : The Saḿskrta word “Kuśt́ha&rdquo; means “skin disease.” So in Saḿskrta prickly heat is also a kind of Kuśt́ha. But what in modern Indian languages we take kuśt́ha (leprosy) to be is called in Saḿskrta, “Bátarakta Roga.”
