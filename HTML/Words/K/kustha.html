Leprosy (3)

[2] The Sanskrit word kuśt́ha means “skin disease.” So in Sanskrit prickly heat is also a kind of kuśt́ha . But what in modern Indian languages we take kuśt́ha [leprosy] to be is called in Sanskrit, bátarakta roga .
