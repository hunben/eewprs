Kakubh to Kavaśńa (Discourse 17) (5)

[146] Kapardaka

[147] By adding ka to the word kaparda we get the word kapardaka. Kapardaka means “the smallest medium [...] thus the colloquial meaning of kapardaka is “cowrie shell”. If we say that someone is kapardakahiina [without kapardaka] it means that they are penniless, or else it means that they don’t have any rags at all.
