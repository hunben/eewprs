[103] Kiilaka

[104] Kiila + kan = kiilaka . Most meanings of the word kiilaka are similar to the meanings [...] word kiila is screw, kiilaka does not mean screw; it means a long nail or peg.

[105] Another meaning of the word kiilaka is pillar. This kind of pillar [...] rub its body against the pillar.

[106] Another meaning of the word kiilaka is nut. Please remember carefully that the bolt is kiila and the nut is kiilaka .
