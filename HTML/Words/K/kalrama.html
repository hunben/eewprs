Karpat́a to Karśú (Discourse 19) (3)

[63] Kalrama

[64] Kalra + amac = kalrama. Its etymological meaning is “one who lets out a sigh while labouring”. Colloquially, kalrama means kalmá or kalmakát́hi dhán [a type of paddy].
