Sándhivigráhika, Janamitram and Lokamitram (3)

[title]

[3] Of the janamitrams in the world, [...] his/her jurisdiction will be known as lokamitram [friend of the world]. The rules for a permanent lokamitram are the same as those for sándhivigráhika and janamitram.
