Ui to Uluka (Discourse 9) (2)

[8] Ukta means “that which has [...] vac + ńic we get the word ucita whose etymological meaning is [...] maintained”. Some people spell the word ucita with t – ucit. This is anucita [improper]. There is no such word as ucit.
