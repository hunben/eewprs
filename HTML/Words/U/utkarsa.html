Aoktha/Aokthaka/Aokthika to Aokśa (Discourse 15) (3)

[39] Utkrśt́a + ań [...] means “excellence” (utkarśa or utkrśt́atá). Adding the suffix ań to utkarśa to form the word aotkarśa is a grammatical error because utkarśa is itself a noun, thus adding the suffix ań to form yet another noun will not do.
