Iigal to Iikśańaka/ Iikśańika/ Iikśańiká (Discourse 8) (2)

[32] In olden times the ivory craft of Bankura and Murshidabad in Bengal was of a very high standard. This craft depended on the patronage of royalty and the wealthy. Nowadays it has nearly disappeared.
