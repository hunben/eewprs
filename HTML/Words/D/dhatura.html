Káca to Kápat́ika (Discourse 21) (5)

[35] Of the ointments which are [...] the best ointments contain the dhaturá [ Datura metal , or thorn apple] as an ingredient. Although the thorny dhaturá is somewhat poisonous as a [...] the golden glow of the body, dhaturá is also called káiṋcana . All types of dhaturá have this medicinal property; but it is primarily found in the black dhaturá .
