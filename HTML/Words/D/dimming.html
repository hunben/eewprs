Prabháta Saḿgiita 2460 (2)

[4] The flowers in my hair have withered. [...] lose You. The light of hope is dimming, Yet I shall never lose heart. The thought of You brings strength to me, O Lord. This dimming lamp of hope is relit with new ghee and wick.
