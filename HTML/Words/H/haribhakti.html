Bhakti and Krpá (4)

[17] This intense love, this tremendous attraction for Viśńu is called Haribhakti. One who possesses Haribhakti is a blessed soul.

[19] (A pariah, who possesses Haribhakti is superior to a Bráhmań, but a vipra devoid of Haribhakti is inferior to a pariah.)
