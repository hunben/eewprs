A Scriptological and Linguistic Survey of the World (5)

[23] Assamese and Bengali horoscopes and books were both written in Sriharsh script, while in Mithila horoscopes were written in Sriharsh and books in Nágrii. In Kashmir, horoscopes were written in Sárada. In the Punjab, during ancient times, horoscopes and books were written in Punjabi Sárada, that is, Saptasindhu Sárada, while today horoscopes are written in Punjabi Sárada and books in Gurumukhi script.
