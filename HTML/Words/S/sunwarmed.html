Kakubh to Kavaśńa (Discourse 17) (4)

[174] Water which is kept in some kind of vessel and sunwarmed is called kavaśńa water. When children are massaged with oil and then bathed in sunwarmed water it increases their vitality. Sunwarmed water is also beneficial for older [...] good if the temperature of the sunwarmed water is greater than the temperature of the body. Ko + uśńa = kavaśńa.
