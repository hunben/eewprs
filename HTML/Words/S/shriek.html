Kit́i to Kiisha (Discourse 26) (2)

[49] If someone touches fire, they will at once let out a shriek. So one name of fire is kiicaka .

[50] If a demon, ghoul or carnivore [...] that animal lets out a plaintive shriek. Therefore the word kiicaka is used for an demon, ghoul or carnovore.
