The Meaning of a Few Names–2 (3)

[7] Sumantra means “a good mantra”, [...] the brahmabiija . Thus sumantra means brahmamantra, brahmabiija . The bliss that one derives with the help of this sumantra or brahmabiija is called sumantrananda .
