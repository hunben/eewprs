Liilá and Kriidá (3)

[1] Ati-snáyukaośika jiṋána (extra-cerebral knowledge) and sútrátmágata jiṋána are two types [...] called anything, but not wise.

[2] Do ati-snáyukaośika jiṋána and sútrátmágata jiṋána come within [...] the scope of relative knowledge?

[3] We call “absolute knowledge” [...] ati-snáyukaośika jiṋána and sútrátmágata jiṋána both are [...] and are not concerned with Him.
