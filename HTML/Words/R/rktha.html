Rk to Rkśa (Discourse 11) (4)

[25] Rktha

[26] By adding the suffix thak to the verbal root rc we get the word rktha. The etymological meaning of the word rktha is “one who is praised by [...] form of wealth. For this reason rktha also means “gold”. [...] open-handed never, never, never.”
