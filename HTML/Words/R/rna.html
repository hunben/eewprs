[50] Rńa

[51] One meaning of the verbal root [...] Rńj/rrńj + kta gives us the word rńa. The etymological meaning of rńa is “that which entangles [...] called uttamarńa (uttama + rńa) and one who takes a loan is called adhamarńa (adhama + rńa). In ancient times rńa used to be spelled with a long rr but both spellings are correct.
