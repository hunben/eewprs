Kulya to Kuvela (Discourse 34) (3)

[9] If we use the word kulya [...] of a large river that ultimately rejoins that river. If a small river joins [...] emerges from the Damodara and later rejoins the Damodara, so its name is Bádái [...] after emerging from the Mahananda, rejoins the Mahananda, so the Kalindii also is kulyá .
