Aoktha/Aokthaka/Aokthika to Aokśa (Discourse 15) (3)

[122] Aorabhra

[124] By adding ań to the root urabhra we get the word aorabhra which means “the thick, [...] woollen products. Both the spellings aorabhra and aorabhrya are correct. Aorabhrya refers only to thick blankets.
