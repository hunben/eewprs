[94] The word alaḿ was also used as an indeclinable. In that case its meaning was “vain” or “futile”. Alaḿ follows this rule when it has [...] vistára is in the third case-ending. Alaḿ múrkhopadeshena – [...] upadesha is in the third case-ending.

[95] Similarly we get the word alaḿkára [ornament], alam + kr + ghaina = alaḿkára, [...] alaḿkarańa has the identical meaning.
