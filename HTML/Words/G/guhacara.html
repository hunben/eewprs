The Intuitional Science of the Vedas – 2 (3)

[37] He is the Life of my life, the [...] so close to me as He. He is the Guhácara or Heart-dweller. Here the word Guhácara does not mean mountain cave-dweller. Here Guhá means heart. Hence the one, who is Guhácara is the heart-dweller – the controlling Lord of the heart.
