Kabala to Kaḿsári (Discourse 20) (3)

[40] 18) If something is affixed tightly to something else, such as a diamond-set ring, a pearl-studded necklace or pearl-studded earring, then the word kavara [...] muktá-kavara aunguriiyaka [pearl-studded ring], hiiraka-kavara mańihára [...] [ruby-inlaid gold-earring], and so on.
